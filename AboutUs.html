At Second Spin, we believe great music shouldn’t break the bank. That’s why we’re dedicated to giving pre loved records a second life. Offering affordable, high-quality and second hand vinyl to music lovers like you.

Why Choose Us?
Affordable Prices – We keep costs low so you can grow your collection without emptying your wallet.
Sustainable Shopping – Buying second-hand helps reduce waste and keeps music spinning for years to come.
Curated Selection – Every record is hand-checked to ensure quality sound at a budget-friendly price.

Our Story
  We started Second Spin as vinyl collectors who love the hunt for rare finds, classics, and hidden gems. Whether you’re a longtime record lover or just starting your collection, we’re here to help you discover amazing music at a fraction of the price.
